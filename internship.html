Internships

Practical work opportunities for recent graduates to gain experience at the Roads Authority.

The Roads Authority occasionally offers internship opportunities for recent graduates seeking practical exposure in fields such as office administration, customer service and other operational departments.

Who can apply

Graduates who have completed an undergraduate qualification in fields relevant to the advertised internship (for example, Bachelor of Business Administration, Bachelor of Business Management, or Bachelor of Office Administration).

Application requirements

  • Certified copies of academic records.
  • Certified copy of your qualification.
  • Certified copy of Namibian ID.
  • Written application letter and detailed CV.
