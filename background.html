ROADS

Background

A brief overview of the Roads Authority's purpose, vision and core values.

Our Vision

A sustainable road sector that supports Namibia's socio-economic goals and Vision 2030.

Our Mission

To manage the national road network so that it is safe, efficient and supports socio-economic growth across Namibia.

Core Values

Our values guide how we work and serve the public.

Respect

We believe that there is strength in diverse teams. Therefore, we respect people from all walks of life.

Open Communication

We believe that people who connect and share information freely are enabled to align towards achieving shared goals.

Accountability

We are uncompromisingly committed to good governance and therefore shall expect employees, at all levels of the organisation, to uphold ethical principles and hold them accountable for their decisions and actions.

Dedication

We are committed to work together as a team in our quest to deliver on our mandate, irrespective of the many obstacles we might face. We will always support one another and never give up.

Service Excellence

We are in pursuit of excellence in everything we do. Therefore, we are always seeking new innovative solutions in providing quality service to our stakeholders.
