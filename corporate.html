About the Roads Authority

According to the Roads Authority Act (Act 17 of 1999), the statutory objective of the Roads Authority is "To manage the national road network so that it is safe and efficient." Notwithstanding this statutory objective, the Roads Authority pursues a strategy aligned with Namibia's 2030 Vision that extends beyond the statutory mandate to support broader national development goals.
