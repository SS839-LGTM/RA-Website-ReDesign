Permits & Regulations

Information on Domestic, Cross-border and Abnormal Load permits, plus download links and application guidance.

Permit Types

Select a permit type to see requirements, processing times and application downloads.

  • Domestic Road Transportation Permit (Public/Private)

    Permits for passenger transport within Namibia (municipal and long-distance). Requirements: ID, vehicle documents, roadworthy certificate, application fee.

    Download Contact

  • Cross-border Transportation Permit

    Permits for international passenger and goods transport. Requirements: business registration, vehicle documents, operator cards.

    Download Contact

  • Abnormal Load Permit

    Required for vehicles exceeding standard size limits. Attach motivation, vehicle specs and route details; processing varies by route.

    Download Contact
