How it works — 4 easy steps

  1. Create a NaTIS Online account (use a secure personal email).
  2. Start the relevant service (renewal, registration, transfer).
  3. Upload required documents and pay fees with the secure payment gateway.
  4. Track the application and follow any instructions or appointments provided.

Security & privacy

NaTIS Online uses secure connections and standard payment providers. Never share your account credentials; verify emails and official links before entering personal information.
