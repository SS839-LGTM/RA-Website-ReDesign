Strategy & Vision

How the Roads Authority aligns its objectives to Namibia’s Vision 2030 and long-term sector goals.

Core Ideology

Our mission is to manage a safe and efficient national road network to support economic growth. Our vision is a sustainable road sector which is ahead of national and regional socio-economic needs in pursuit of Namibia's Vision 2030.

Strategic priorities

  • Infrastructure preservation and maintenance
  • Safety and compliance
  • Data-driven planning (RMS & BMS)
  • Capacity building and stakeholder engagement
