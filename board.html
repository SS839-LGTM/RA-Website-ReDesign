Board & Governance (summary)

The Board oversees RA's long-term strategy, financial sustainability and corporate governance. The current Board comprises seven independent non-executive directors (appointed effective 01 September 2025) and is supported by three sub-committees: Audit, Risk & Compliance; Human Capital; and Roads Network Strategy & Projects.

For enquiries about board meetings or governance documents contact the PR office at pr@ra.org.na or use the site's contact form.
