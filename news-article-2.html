New NATIS Centre Opens in Opuwo

29 June 2014 — Opuwo

The Roads Authority has opened a new Namibia Traffic Information System (NATIS) centre in Opuwo to improve services in the Kunene Region. The centre provides vehicle registration, licence issuance and permit support for local communities.

Opening hours and contact details are available on the main site - residents are encouraged to visit during weekdays for permit and enquiry services.
