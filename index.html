Namibia's Road Network

The Roads Authority is the custodian of the road network on behalf of the Government of Namibia.

Road Management System

All proclamations and data regarding Namibia's road network are deposited in the Road Management System (RMS) database. This system ensures efficient planning, maintenance, and development of road infrastructure.
