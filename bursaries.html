Bursaries

Support for Namibian students pursuing studies aligned with RA's needs.

The Roads Authority offers annual bursaries to Namibian nationals pursuing full-time tertiary studies in fields required by the organisation.

Who should apply

Namibian nationals who meet the entry requirements of their chosen tertiary institution and the course of study (e.g., civil engineering, IT, or related disciplines).

Application requirements

  • Completed application form (download where available).
  • Certified copies of academic records and qualifications.
  • Certified copy of Namibian ID.
  • Written motivation and any supporting documents.

Bursary benefits

Bursaries typically cover tuition fees, accommodation, meals and a book allowance. Bursary recipients are expected to meet academic progress requirements for renewal.

Selection and obligations

Selection is based on academic merit, interviews and the needs of the Authority. Recipients are required to render service to the Roads Authority for a period commensurate with the duration of the bursary, and poor academic performance can result in termination of the award.

Where to send applications

Applications should be addressed to:
The Head: Learning, OD & Performance, Roads Authority
Private Bag 12030, Ausspannplatz, Windhoek.

Closing date for bursary applications is generally 30 September each academic year.
