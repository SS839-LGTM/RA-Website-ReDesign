Procurement Plan

Roads Authority procurement planning documents and expected upcoming procurement opportunities.

The Roads Authority publishes an Annual Procurement Plan summarising planned procurement activity for the year. Local copies of recent plans are available below.

Annual Procurement Plans — click Download to save the PDF
Year Document Summary Download
2020–2021 Annual Procurement Plan 2020–2021

The 2020–2021 Annual Procurement Plan sets out planned procurement activities focused on roads maintenance and operational support. It lists anticipated tenders, procurement methods (e.g., open tender, request for quotations), and indicative timelines to guide both internal planning and supplier preparation. Use this plan to identify likely tender categories and expected procurement windows.

 Download
2021–2022 Annual Procurement Plan 2021–2022

The 2021–2022 plan details planned capital and maintenance projects, anticipated procurement categories and expected timelines. It provides a forecast of tendering activity to help suppliers plan resources and ensure readiness for upcoming opportunities. The plan supports transparency and assists internal budget alignment.

 Download
2022–2023 Annual Procurement Plan 2022–2023

The 2022–2023 plan provides a high-level forecast of procurement activity including capital works, maintenance programmes and equipment purchases. It highlights priority projects, procurement methods and indicative timing to inform vendors and internal stakeholders. Suppliers are encouraged to review the plan to assess opportunity relevance and prepare compliance documentation.

 Download
2023–2024 Annual Procurement Plan 2023–2024

This plan outlines procurement priorities for 2023–2024, including projected tenders for major capital and service contracts. It provides estimated timelines and preferred procurement routes to help the market anticipate upcoming opportunities. Use the plan to align supplier capability and procurement readiness with the Authority's schedule.

 Download
2024–2025 Annual Procurement Plan 2024–2025

The 2024–2025 plan consolidates planned procurement across departments, indicating priority works and schedules for the forthcoming year. It lists expected procurement categories, approximate timing, and intended procurement approaches to support transparent market engagement. Prospective suppliers should review the plan to identify relevant opportunities and plan submissions.

 Download
2025–2026 Annual Procurement Plan 2025–2026

The 2025–2026 plan outlines procurement categories and key activities for the coming financial year, focusing on maintenance programmes, capital rehabilitation, and service contracts. It provides a high-level forecast of tenders, estimated timelines and recommended procurement methods to assist vendor planning. Use this plan to prepare capacity, compliance documents, and bid readiness.
