A Personalized Licence Number (PLN) is a registration chosen by the applicant consisting of up to seven alpha-numeric characters followed by the national flag and the letters "NA". PLNs must not be obscene, misleading or offensive and are subject to approval by NaTIS.

  • Completed PLN application form (available at NaTIS offices or via NaTIS Online).
  • Certified copy of Namibian ID or passport.
  • Proof of vehicle ownership (if transferring) or authorisation for third-party applications.
  • Two or three preferred plate choices in order of priority.

How to apply (summary)

  1. Prepare required documents and choose 3 preferred plate options.
  2. Submit application at a NaTIS office or via NaTIS Online.
  3. Wait for approval notification; approved PLNs must be paid for within the time specified.
  4. Collect plates once manufactured and verify the registration details.

Notes & restrictions

PLNs are subject to the Roads Authority's approval and must not contravene national regulations. Obscene, misleading or reserved combinations will be rejected. Reservation periods are limited — check with your NaTIS office.
