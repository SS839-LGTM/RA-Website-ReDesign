Quick Actions

Start an application, book a test, or download the forms you need.

Learner Licence

Apply for a learner licence and prepare for the learner test. Fees apply for application and issuing.

Renewal / Replacement

Renew an expiring licence or replace a lost/expired licence. Additional documentation may be required depending on the lapse.

Professional Driver's Permit (PDP)

Apply for professional authorisation for commercial drivers. Medical, police and training certificates may be required.

How to book & take the test

  1. Complete the required application form and attach certified ID.
  2. Pay the booking fee at your registering authority — test dates are provided on the receipt.
  3. Attend the test with the vehicle & documentation; if the vehicle passes the roadworthy test, a certificate will be issued.
  4. Contact your local NaTIS centre to confirm available test dates and booking process.

Booking fees may vary (example: N$150) and roadworthy issuing fee example is N$100 — confirm with your local NaTIS office.

Frequently required documents

  • Certified Namibian ID or traffic register number
  • Copy of expired or current licence (for renewal)
  • Medical certificate for applicants over 65 or for professional permits
  • Certificate of training (for dangerous goods drivers)
